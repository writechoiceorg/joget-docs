In the Joget ecosystem, you find various apps ranging from simple database-driven ones to more complex approval workflows and comprehensive solutions like Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems. Apps are web applications designed to run on the Joget platform. These apps seamlessly integrate workflow management, data administration, and robust reporting capabilities, empowering your digital transformation journey.

You have the freedom to personalize or craft your very own version of the App Center. Refer to the [Customizing App Center](https://dev.joget.org/community/display/DX8/Customizing+App+Center) guide for detailed information on customizing the App Center.

### Step 1 - Accessing the App Center

The App Center is the default landing page within Joget DX, showcasing a grid of published apps designed to enhance your efficiency and innovation. To access the App Center, use a web browser to navigate to your Joget server. To access it, use the following link [http://localhost:8080/jw](http://localhost:8080/jw). If you have used different host or port during the instalation, change the URL.

### Step 2 - Login

Use the **Login** button on the left to access the login page and access the platform as a user. Each user's experience is tailored, granting access to distinct apps based on individual permissions.

## Install Apps from Joget Marketplace

The Joget Marketplace provides an easy way to integrate new apps into your Joget instance, expanding your toolkit. You can access the Joget Marketplace within the App Center's by selecting **Download from Marketplace**.

<Images
  imgURL="https://ajeuwbhvhr.cloudimg.io/colony-recorder.s3.amazonaws.com/files/2023-08-28/3f40438d-bb65-456c-8c20-4bccc0921dd4/ascreenshot.jpeg?tl_px=604,95&br_px=1679,696&force_format=png&wat_scale=95&wat=1&wat_opacity=0.7&wat_gravity=northwest&wat_url=https://colony-recorder.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/images/watermarks/FB923C_standard.png&wat_pad=502,265"

/>

:::info Access Joget Marketplace
You don't need a Joget Marketplace account to access it. However, you'll need to register for a separate Joget Marketplace account to be able to download and install apps.
:::

After accessing the Joget Marketplace, you can search for apps that fulfill your needs and select them for additional information.

## Templates

Templates is a new feature introduced in Joget DX 8 that allows app designers to template existing apps by modifying entity names in the app design. To use the Templates solution, use the steps below:

1. From the **App Center**, access templates by selecting the **Templates** option.
